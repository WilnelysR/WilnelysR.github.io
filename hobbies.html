Painting

I've always loved painting, and it became an outlet for me to escape reality for a little. I dont think I'm any good, but its definitely therapy for me.

Cricut

Another hobby that I enjoy doing is making decals and other things with my Cricut. The Cricut is a die-cutting machine. Think of it like a printer: you create an image or design on your computer and then send it to the machine. This was another creative outlet for me to just decompress and let my creativity flow. So far I've only made decals, but I am starting to explore more with all the things that I can make.
